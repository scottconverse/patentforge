# Summary of the Invention

Generate the "Summary of the Invention" section for a patent application.

This section must:
1. Briefly state the general nature and purpose of the invention (1 paragraph)
2. Summarize the key aspects of the invention that address the problems identified in the Background (2-3 paragraphs)
3. Reference the independent claims in general terms without claim numbers
4. Highlight the advantages and improvements over prior approaches

The summary should be broader than the claims but narrower than the Background. It bridges the gap between the problem (Background) and the detailed solution (Detailed Description).

Do NOT:
- Copy claim language verbatim
- Include specific implementation details (save for Detailed Description)
- Use language that could narrow the claim scope
