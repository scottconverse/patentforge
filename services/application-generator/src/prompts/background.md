# Background of the Invention

Generate the "Background of the Invention" section for a patent application.

This section must:
1. Describe the technical field of the invention (1 paragraph)
2. Describe the state of the prior art and existing approaches (2-4 paragraphs)
3. Identify the problems, limitations, or deficiencies of existing approaches (1-2 paragraphs)
4. Optionally reference specific prior art patents if provided

The background should naturally lead the reader to understand why the invention was needed, without directly describing the invention itself.

Do NOT:
- Disparage prior art (use neutral language like "there remains a need for" instead of "the prior art fails to")
- Describe the present invention in this section
- Make admissions about the prior art that could be used against the application
