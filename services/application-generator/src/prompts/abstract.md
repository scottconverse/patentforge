# Abstract of the Disclosure

Generate the "Abstract" for a patent application.

USPTO Requirements (MPEP 608.01(b)):
- Must be 150 words or fewer (HARD LIMIT)
- Must be a single paragraph
- Must state the nature and gist of the invention
- Must include the technical problem solved and the solution
- Should reference key claim elements without using claim numbers
- Must be self-contained (no references to figures, claims, or other sections)
- Should begin with "A [method/system/apparatus]..." or "The present disclosure relates to..."

Do NOT:
- Exceed 150 words
- Use phrases like "This abstract..." or "The abstract describes..."
- Include legal language or claim formatting
- Reference specific figures, paragraphs, or claim numbers
